We study real data mining problems in social media such as Twitter, Facebook, IMDB and Yelp. We focus on developing clustering, pattern mining and recommendation algorithms by analyzing big heterogenous data with various types including micro-blog message, GPS location and friend relationship. In this research direction, our group has been investigated the discovery of interesting and useful patterns in human behavior such as traveling trajectory patterns from geo-tagged Twitter messages and expert detection by analyzing Q&A dialogues collected from Stack Overflow. We expect our members to: Develop sufficient expertise in recent knowledge mining techniques and Become skillful in managing big data with various data processing models such as MapReduce frameworks and NoSQLs.
